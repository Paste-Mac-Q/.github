## Overview

Paste Advanced is a beautiful clipboard manager that replaces the single-item clipboard with a rich visual history of everything you have copied, organized in a searchable, iCloud-synced library with pinboards for frequently reused content.

The visual history interface displays recent clipboard items as a scrollable gallery of previews, making it fast to identify and reuse any previously copied text, image, URL, or file by appearance rather than requiring exact text recall.

Pinboards organize frequently used content into named collections that persist independently of the clipboard history, providing a permanent library of standard responses, code snippets, design assets, and any other content you reuse regularly.

iCloud sync keeps your clipboard history and pinboards consistent across all your devices, ensuring that content copied on one device is available on another without manual transfer, making cross-device workflows seamless.

For anyone who copies and pastes throughout their work and wants their clipboard to be a searchable, organized library of all their recent content rather than a single forgotten item, Paste Advanced is the clipboard manager that transforms how reused content is managed.

## Performance

Paste balances performance with system resource usage intelligently. Operations are handled in the background, keeping your Mac responsive for everything else. Efficient memory management and smart caching reduce overhead during extended work sessions.

Apple Silicon acceleration is fully utilized on M-series Macs, delivering noticeably faster performance. On Intel machines, hardware acceleration is used wherever available.
